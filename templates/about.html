Our Mission

The AIResQ ClimSols Flood Monitoring Portal is dedicated to leveraging technology and community participation for effective disaster response. Our crowdsourced flood monitoring platform enables real-time data collection to help emergency responders, government agencies, and communities make informed decisions during flood emergencies.

How It Works

1

Report

Citizens report flood depths using visual reference objects (cars, bikes, persons) and upload GPS-tagged photos

2

Verify

Reports are collected, verified by officials, and validated for accuracy

3

Analyze

Data is analyzed in real-time to identify critical flood zones and trends

4

Respond

Emergency services and volunteers coordinate response efforts based on data

Key Features

GPS-tagged flood reports
Photo evidence verification
Real-time flood mapping
Volunteer coordination
Multi-language support
Real-time analytics
